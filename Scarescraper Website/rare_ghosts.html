Quick description on how this floor works:

For each 5F, 10F, and 20F, you will encounter some Rare Ghosts which each have special abilities from their original varient. Each Rare Ghost floor will have one new Rare Ghost. On 5F, you will start with one predetermined ghost at random. On 10F, you will get one new ghost plus the previous ghost from 5F. And on 15F, you will get one more additional ghost plus the previous ghosts from 5F and 10F.

Each ghost that you get is determined by the player count (excluding Gooigi) at 4:59 and the number of ghosts you need to capture. To find out how to figure out what ghost you'll get, click the Calculate Ghost button.
